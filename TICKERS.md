# Tickers you can use in the Stock Price Predictor App

You can enter any valid Yahoo Finance ticker. Below are popular examples across equities, ETFs, indexes, crypto, commodities, and FX.

Notes
- Exchange suffixes matter (examples): 
  - India NSE: .NS, BSE: .BO
  - UK LSE: .L
  - Canada TSX: .TO
  - Germany XETRA: .DE
  - France Paris: .PA
  - Netherlands Amsterdam: .AS
  - Japan TSE: .T
  - Hong Kong: .HK
  - Australia ASX: .AX
  - Korea KOSPI: .KS, KOSDAQ: .KQ
  - Taiwan TWSE: .TW
  - China Shanghai: .SS, Shenzhen: .SZ
- Index symbols often start with ^ (e.g., ^GSPC).
- Crypto pairs use the “-USD” suffix (e.g., BTC-USD).
